Why only {{ settings.MAX_CONNECTIONS_PER_USER }} connections?

FriendZone is a tool for maintaining the real relationships in your life. Humans cannot maintain an unlimited number of relationships, so having a very large number of connections indicates that someone is using FriendZone outside its intended purpose.

FriendZone is explicitly not for the following purposes:

  • Advertising.
  • Meeting new people.
  • Maximizing likes, numbers of "friends", or engagement.

Limiting the number of connections our users can have prevents people from abusing FriendZone for profit or competition.

For more information on FriendZone's choice to limit connections, see Dunbar's number.
